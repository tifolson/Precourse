* `for`:
    use to set up a loop where you can repeat the same thing over again 
    that way you don't have to re-write the same instructions multiple times

* `&&`, `||`, `!`
    && : AND ---> means that both conditions must be met for something else to happen
    || : OR ---> means that one condition or the other must be met for something else to happen
    ! : NOT ---> means that something cannot happen in order for something else to happen
